IMPORTANT: Clear Browser Cache
==========================================

The material dropdown issue you're seeing is likely due to browser caching.
Please follow these steps to clear your cache:

OPTION 1: Force Refresh (Quick)
--------------------------------
1. Open Design Slayer in your browser
2. Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
3. This forces a hard refresh and bypasses the cache

OPTION 2: Developer Tools (Recommended)
----------------------------------------
1. Open Design Slayer
2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
3. Right-click the refresh button
4. Select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"

OPTION 3: Clear All Browser Cache
----------------------------------
1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Choose time range "Last hour" or "All time"
4. Click "Clear data"
